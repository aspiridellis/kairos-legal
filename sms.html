Kairos — Accountability Text Messages

Kairos is a personal screen-time app operated by Alexander Spiridellis (Sole Proprietor). This page describes our accountability-partner SMS program and how recipients opt in.

What the program is

A Kairos user can invite one accountability partner — a person they know — to receive a short daily text summarizing which of the user's own self-set app limits they went over that day. Messages are personal accountability notifications only. No marketing, promotional, or third-party content is ever sent.

How recipients opt in (two-step confirmation)

  1. Inside the Kairos app, the user enters the mobile number of someone they personally know and asks Kairos to invite them.
  2. The recipient receives one confirmation text and must reply YES to opt in. No further messages are sent unless they reply YES. Their YES reply is stored as their express consent.

Example messages

kairos: alex added you as their accountability partner. reply YES to start getting their daily check-in, STOP to decline.
kairos: alex today went over their limits: instagram (2h) · tiktok (1h). reply STOP to opt out.

Frequency, opt-out, and rates

Up to one message per day, only on days a limit is exceeded. Reply STOP at any time to stop all messages, or HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.

Privacy & terms

Phone numbers are never shared with third parties other than our SMS provider (Twilio). See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
