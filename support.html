Common questions:

How do I cancel my subscription? Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Kairos → Cancel Subscription. Or from inside Kairos: Settings → Manage Subscription.

Why isn't blocking working? Make sure Screen Time access is granted: Kairos Settings → Permissions, or check iOS Settings → Screen Time.
